## Design Context

### Users

Primary audience is course graders and instructors reviewing an ML systems project demo.
Users need to quickly understand model workflow and verify that anomaly decisions are both accurate and explainable.

### Brand Personality

Calm, polished, and technically credible.
The interface should feel reliable and clear rather than flashy.

### Aesthetic Direction

Light, research-demo presentation with green-toned scientific/engineering cues.
Motion should be smooth and continuous on modern laptops, without distracting effects.
For reduced-motion users, behavior should be almost fully static.

### Design Principles

1. Prioritize clarity of model evidence over decorative effects.
2. Use motion to connect sections and state changes, not to entertain.
3. Keep transitions short, smooth, and purposeful.
4. Preserve responsiveness and perceived speed on laptop-first targets.
5. Respect accessibility, especially prefers-reduced-motion, by minimizing movement.
